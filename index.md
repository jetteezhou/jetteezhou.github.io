## About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student jointly trained by Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) and the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Jinqiao Wang](https://nlpr.ia.ac.cn/iva/homepage/jqwang/index.htm), Prof. [Honghui Dong](https://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/8022/) and Dr. [Chaoyang Zhao](https://ia.cas.cn/rcdw/fyjy/202404/t20240422_7129867.html). I am currently a Research Intern under the *Tencent "Project UP"* (腾讯青云计划) at *Tencent Robotics X Lab*, supervised by Dr. [Zhengyou Zhang](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%BC%A0%E6%AD%A3%E5%8F%8B). 

My research interests lie in **Embodied AI** and **Foundation Models for Robotics**. Currently, my work focuses on endowing robots with physical common sense through **Physically-grounded VLMs** and exploring **World Models** for predictive control and long-horizon reasoning.
