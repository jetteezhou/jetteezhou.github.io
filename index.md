## About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student jointly trained by <span class="highlight-company">Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU)</span> and the <span class="highlight-company">Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA)</span>. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Jinqiao Wang](https://nlpr.ia.ac.cn/iva/homepage/jqwang/index.htm), Prof. [Honghui Dong](https://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/8022/) and Dr. [Chaoyang Zhao](https://ia.cas.cn/rcdw/fyjy/202404/t20240422_7129867.html). 

I was previously a Research Intern at <span class="highlight-company">Tencent Robotics X Lab</span> (腾讯Robotics X实验室) under the leadership of Dr. [Zhengyou Zhang](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E5%BC%A0%E6%AD%A3%E5%8F%8B), where I worked on the [TAIROS](https://tairos.tencent.com/) embodied AI platform and multi-agent shared memory reasoning.

My research goal is to build generalist agents capable of cognizing and interacting with the physical world. My primary research interests include:
- **Embodied Spatial Reasoning**: Embodied cognition, spatial understanding, and active visual reasoning in physical environments.
- **Unified Perception-Generation-Action Modeling**: Closed-loop integration of understanding, generation, and action for embodied agents.
- **Multimodal Large Language Models**: Post-training alignment, Reinforcement Learning (RL), and world models (VLM, World Model). Lightweight LLM applications in task planning.
